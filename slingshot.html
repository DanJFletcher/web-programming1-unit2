SLINGSHOT

What is the Slingshot?

The Slingshot is a water purification system that uses vapor compression to distill water no matter how polluted. Kamen's ambitioun was to bring affordable clean water to impoverished areas of the world. According to World Health Organization, there are 900 million people worldwide without access to safe drinking water and 3.5 million people die each year because of it.

Some of the goals of the machine is: lasting five years of operations without maintenance, using less electricity than a microwave oven, and generating 1000 liters of pure water per day.
