SEGWAY

The Segway

If anyone knows who Dean Kamen is, it will most likely be due to the Segway. Kamen was already a successful inventor before the Segway having already invented the iBOT, the first drug infusion pump, patents for technology used to make portable dialyses machines, and an insulin pump.

The Segway, unfortunately failed to live up to the hype of the media and inevitbly flopped. It simply doesn't fit well into the current infrastructure of any city today. Some countries even banned the Segway from sidewalks and roads. Maybe one day, when we have cities without cars, the Segway will be regarded as an innovation ahead of its time.
