Dean Kamen

American Engineer / Inventor / World Changer

Who is Dean Kamen?

Dean Kamen is the man! Perhaps best know for inventing the Segway, he is also the founder and organiser of FIRST, an international robitics and engineering competition. He created the Slingshot which is a water purification system designed to bring clean, affordable water to places with no access to proper drinking water.

This website is in tribute to all the hardwork and dedication Dean Kamen has done for the world. The scope of his work couldn't possibly be summarized in this website - let alone a couple paragraphs! Visit the other pages on this here website to learn more about his awesomeness.
