AWARDS

Awards

Among the few projects mentioned on this tribute site, Dean Kamen has also recieved many awards for his work and contributions to society including: the National Inventors Hall of Fame, Global Humanitarian Action Award by the UN, ASME Medal, IRI Achievement Award, and the Benjamin Franklin Medal in Mechanical Engineering.

Honorable Degrees

Kamen also recieved many honarary degrees including: honorary "Doctor of Enginneering" degrees from Renselaer Polytechnic Insititue, and Kettering University. Honorary "Doctor of Science" degrees from Clarkson University and University of Arizona. Honorary doctrates from Wentworth Institute of Technology, North Carolina State University, Bates College, Georgia Institute of Technology, Illinois Institute of Technology and Plymouth State University. He also received an honorary "Doctor of Engineering and Technology" degree from Yale.

You can see the full list of awards Kamen has on his Wikipedia Page.
