FIRST

What is FIRST?

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a global organization founded by Dean Kamen, with the goal of promoting STEM careers in young people. FIRST brings a sports-like atmosphere to the domain of Science, Technology, and Engineering (STEM).

FIRST Competitions

FIRST organization has few competitions, each geared for different age groups. FIRST LEGO Jr. is a partnership between LEGO and FIRST for younge students ages 6 to 10. FIRST LEGO League is for ages 9 to 14 and involves more advanced challenges. FIRST Tech Challenge if for grades 7 to 12, and students build, program and operate real robots. FIRST Robotics Competition is for high school students, and involves building and competing with industrial sized robots.
